可能（程响）

|Word Count:632|Reading Time:2mins|Post Views:

可能南方的阳光 照着北方的风

Perhaps the southern sunshine falls on the northern wind

可能时光被吹走 从此无影无踪

Maybe time has been blown away, disappeared without a trace

可能故事只剩下 一个难忘的人

Possibly the story only leaves behind an unforgettable person

可能在昨夜梦里 依然笑得纯真

In last night’s dream, you still laughed innocently

可能北京的后海 许多漂泊的魂

Perhaps there are many wandering souls in Beijing’s Houhai

可能成都小酒馆 有群孤独的人

Lonely people sitting in the small Chengdu tavern

可能枕边有微笑 才能暖你清晨

A smile next to your pillow can warm your morning

可能夜空有流星 才能照你前行

The night sky with shooting stars can light your way

可能西安城墙上 有人誓言不分

On the walls of Xi’an, there are people pledging undying love

可能要去到大理 才算爱得认真

Only by going to Dali can you truly get love

可能谁说要陪你 牵手走完一生

Who said they would accompany you for a lifetime

可能笑着流出泪 某天在某时辰

Laughing out tears, someday, at some moment

可能桂林有渔船 为你迷茫点灯

There may be fishing boats in Guilin to guide you when you are lost

可能在呼伦草原 牛羊流成风景

Cattle and sheep on the Hulunbuir grasslands

可能再也找不到 愿意相信的人

Perhaps you can never find someone willing to believe

可能穿越了彷徨 脚步才能坚定

Only by passing through confusion can your steps become firm

可能武当山道上 有人虔诚攀登

On the path of Wudang Mountain, someone is climbing devoutly

可能周庄小巷里 忽然忘掉年轮

In the alleys of Zhouzhuang, suddenly forgetting about the years

可能要多年以后 才能看清曾经

It may take many years to see clearly what happened in the past

可能在当时身边 有双温柔眼晴

A pair of tender and gentle eyes by your side at that time

可能西安城墙上 有人誓言不分

On the walls of Xi’an, there are people pledging undying love

可能要去到大理 才算爱得认真

Only by going to Dali can you truly get love

可能谁说要陪你 牵手走完一生

Who said they would accompany you for a lifetime

可能笑着流出泪

Laughing out tears

可能终于有一天 刚好遇见爱情

One day, you may finally happen to encounter love

可能永远在路上 有人奋斗前行

Some people always strive forward on the road

可能一切的可能 相信才有可能

All possibilities can only be realized by believing

可能拥有过梦想 才能叫做青春

Only by having dreams can you call it youth
